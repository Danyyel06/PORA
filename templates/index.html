What You Can Learn

Data Science & Artificial Intelligence

A track that provides an overview of data science concepts, including data analysis, data visualization, and machine learning.

Software Product Management

A track focused on understanding the principles of software product management and how to bring software products to market

Project Manangement

A compulsory course that provides an introduction to project management principles and methodologies.

DevOPs

A track that focuses on merging software development and operations, fostering collaboration and automation for efficient, continuous software delivery, enhancing speed, quality, and adaptability.

Foreign Languages

Foreign languages help to broaden perspectives, enabling cross-cultural communication. Learning any of them enhances cognitive skills.

A track that focuses on the design, development, and delivery of software systems and applications.

A track dedicated to understanding the principles of cyber security and how to secure and protect information systems.
